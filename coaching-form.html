About Therapeutic Coaching

Therapeutic coaching at Lavender Health is delivered by Michelle Burke, who holds a Therapeutic Coaching Diploma (Burrell Education) and is a BACP Student Member currently completing a Level 4 Integrative Counselling qualification.
